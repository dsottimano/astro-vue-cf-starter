Spring has reliably been the busiest stretch of the housing calendar, and this
year is no exception. Inventory is up year over year, which gives buyers more
room to negotiate than they've had in a while.

If you're shopping this season, get your financing in order early and be ready
to move quickly on the homes that fit.
