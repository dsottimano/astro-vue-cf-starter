# Blog posts

[← Back to wiki](index.md)

Posts are articles/news shown in the blog. Open **Posts** 📝 in the left menu.

## Write a post

1. Click **Posts → Add**.
2. Choose the **language**.
3. Fill in the fields:
   - **Title** — the headline. The **slug** (web address) auto-fills from it.
   - **Translation key** — links this post to its other-language version
     (auto-filled; leave as-is unless pairing translations).
   - **Status** — **Draft** (hidden) or **Published** (public).
   - **Publish date** — the date shown on the article and used for ordering.
   - **Excerpt** — a short summary for listing/preview cards (optional).
   - **Cover image** — the lead image (see [Photos & media](media-photos.md)).
   - **Categories** — tick the categories this post belongs to (see
     [Categories](categories.md)).
   - **Body** — the article itself, written in **Markdown**.
4. Click **Save**, then wait a minute or two for it to appear
   ([How publishing works](publishing.md)).

## A 60-second Markdown primer

The body uses Markdown — plain text with a few simple marks:

```
## A subheading

A normal paragraph. Make text **bold** or *italic*.

- a bullet
- another bullet

[a link](https://example.com)
```

You don't need to memorize it — plain paragraphs work fine on their own.

## Draft vs. Published

- **Draft** — saved but **not shown** on the public blog. Use it for work in
  progress.
- **Published** — visible to everyone (after the rebuild).

Switch a post from Draft to Published when it's ready, then Save.

## Edit or delete

Open **Posts**, click the article to edit it, or use the delete control in the
list. All changes are versioned and appear after the next rebuild.
